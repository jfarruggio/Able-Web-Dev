The Caleffi Exellence contest runs for 4 quaters. July 1st, 2013 to July 30th, 2014.

Piotr Zelasko Able Distributors

First Quater Grand Prize Winner

Are you proud of your installation design? If so Caleffi North America wants to help you show it off. You could win a trip to Italy as well!

Beginning this month and continuing for a year we are asking designers, contractors and engineers to show us their proudest jobs that feature our products in the Caleffi Excellence contest. Each quarter (for four quarters) Caleffi will give away iPad™ minis to the four entrants receiving the most votes from the public. The 16 winning entrants will also be welcomed to discuss their work with Bob Rohr in a future Coffee with Caleffi webinar.


Additionally from each quarter’s four top contestants, a grand prize winner will be chosen. Selected from a panel of industry experts and Caleffi executives, the four grand prize winners and their guests will win a trip to Italy to see Caleffi World Headquarters and receive a personalized tour of our factories and design facilities.


It’s easy to enter your design. Here you will write a summary of the design and upload photos. Clear photos are helpful for others to understand your work. Add as much description as you desire including design objectives, what you did, why, and results. What should your design focus be? Well it could center on energy efficiency, resource conservation, system performance, cost savings/avoidance, renewable energy, problem solving, minimizing downtime, comfort, etc. Basically any approach that addressed your customer’s needs and Caleffi product had a role in. Check out this example entry to serve as a guide for you.


After each quarter’s entry registration period ends and the public open voting period begins, all entrants will receive a personalized e-mail from Caleffi containing details of your design. You can use this e-mail to communicate your work to colleagues, trade associates and any others you want to encourage to vote for you. Convenient links will also be included for communicating via social networks Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Google+.
Buona Fortuna! (Good Luck)
